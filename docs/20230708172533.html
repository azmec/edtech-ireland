Schools I.T. 2000

Schools I.T. 2000 is a public policy framework regarding the integration of technologies in schools published by the Irish Department of Education in 1998. As evidenced by the publication, Ireland justified educational technologies for their supposed economic and catalytic potentials and, inadvertently, neglect the pedagogical potential of it.
