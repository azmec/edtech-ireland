Digital Strategy for Schools to 2027

Digital Strategy for Schools to 2027 is a 2022 policy publication produced by the Irish Department of Education and, when compared to its predecessors, represents a radical repositioning of educational technology as a tool to be integrated with and valued for sound pedagogic practice rather economic motivations.

Bears great similarity with the modern educational technology policy of the United States, as represented by [[Reimagining the Role of Technology in Education]].
