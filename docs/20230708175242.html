Irish Assessment Software Enterprises

The act of integrating technology into assessment processes, within the DES’ value system, represents both a streamlining of the education process and an enhancement of sound practice. Ireland’s edtech industry is home to many enterprises providing assessment software frameworks through learning management systems or attempting to revolutionize the process through unique technologies, but each remain in the business of aiding educators in their documenting of student knowledge. Though vast, such enterprises will be sampled by two: JumpAGrade and TestReach.

JumpAGrade enhances assessment by using machine learning to construct formative assessments of students in grind schools. It is funded by Enterprise Ireland and research-backed by Learnovate. JumpAGrade exemplifies not only the government’s interest in edtech but also edtech’s placement within the Irish education system.
